Step 2

Add an extension

Make sure to add your name to the meta tag in the head of the index.html file so that we know who to enter into the prize draw.

Next, enable and use a Netlify Extension to enter the competition and to reveal the next steps which will earn you more points for a better chance of winning.

Here's how:

  1. Install the netlify-edge-includes-at-compose extension for your Netlify team.
  2. Once installed, you can use it on any of your sites. Enable it for this site from the Netlify UI under Site configuration > General.
  3. The extension will let you inject content into your site via a component. Add the code shown below to this site's index.html and push your changes to trigger another site deployment to to see the result. 
    <netlify-edge-include href="https://compose-challenge.netlify.app/partials/add-image-cdn"></netlify-edge-include>
  4. Follow the instructions included into the next deployment of your site.
